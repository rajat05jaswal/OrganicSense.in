About Organic Sense

We envision to become a positive catalyst in offering healthy and sustainable solutions to the society by providing safe, zero-calorie natural sugar substitute through our Stevia based products.

Organic Sense is a vertically integrated producer of zero calorie, natural sweetener Stevia. We cultivate Stevia across different parts of India through our channel partners and work towards achieving our goal of sustainable Stevia production. We at Organic Sense, specialize in providing Stevia extract and Stevia leaves and cater to the global food and beverage industry.
