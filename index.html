Zero Calories & Great Taste

Zero calorie sweetener Stevia, is around 200-300 times sweeter than sugar and the sweet taste is due to the compounds called steviol glycosides which are present in the leaf. The growing concerns about diabetes, hypertension and obesity are gradually making the usage of Stevia popular worldwide and it has been approved by all the major regulatory authorities present globally. Presently many food and beverage companies are already using this great tasting, zero calorie sweetener as an alternative for sugar and artificial sweeteners.

Stevia Origin

Stevia Rebaudiana is a zero calorie, plant based sweetener native to South America. It was discovered hundreds of years ago in Paraguay, where the natives used it to sweeten beverages. The steviol glycosides, mainly Steviosides and Rebaudiosides present in the leaf are responsible for the sweetness of the leaf and have a sugar like taste. These glycosides are present in both dried Stevia leaves and the Stevia extract.

Sure Stevia TM

A healthy and a natural alternative to sugar and artificial sweeteners. Sure Stevia products are sustainably produced and are available as dry Stevia leaves, liquid drops and Stevia extract. The easy to use, dry Stevia leaves are 20-30 times sweeter than sugar and the Stevia extract which looks like fine sugar in appearance, is 200-300 times sweeter than sugar.
